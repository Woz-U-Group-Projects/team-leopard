Steak and Sugar Snap Peas in Ginger Sauce

10m
Prep Time

12m
Cook Time

Ingredients 4 Servings
1 pound skirt steak, cut into 2-inch strips
1 tablespoon corn starch
4 tablespoons oil
1 tablespoon Garlic
1 tablespoon Ginger
1 medium yellow onion, sliced
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1/2 cup beef broth
2 cups sugar snap peas, trimmed
