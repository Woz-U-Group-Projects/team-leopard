Grilled Lemon Basil Chicken
10m
Prep Time

15m
Cook Time

Ingredients 8 Servings
½ cup white wine
¼ cup olive oil
1 tablespoon Gourmet Garden Lemongrass, Stir-In Paste
1 tablespoon Gourmet Garden Basil, Stir-In Paste
1 tablespoon Gourmet Garden Garlic, Stir-In Paste
1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts

Preparation
Combine wine, olive oil, Gourmet Garden Lemongrass Stir-In Paste, Gourmet Garden Basil Stir-In Paste and Gourmet Garden Garlic Stir-In Paste; mix well.

Place chicken in large resealable plastic bag. Add Lemongrass mixture. Seal bag and refrigerate for one hour.

Preheat grill to High. Remove chicken from marinade; discard marinade. Grill chicken 2 minutes per side. Reduce heat to medium or move chicken to coller part of grill. Grill 5-8 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 160 degress.
