GLUTEN FREE WAFFLES WITH BERRY YOGURT TOPPING

INGREDIENTS:
– Pure Maple Syrup
– California Butter
– 1 package store bought Frozen Waffles
Berry Yogurt Topping:
– 1 cup California Plain Yogurt
– 1/4 cup Fruit Sweetened Jam (Strawberry or Raspberry)
– Toasted Walnuts
– Fresh Fruit

DIRECTIONS:
Prepare waffles.

Combine yogurt with berry jam.

Top hot waffles with butter & maple syrup.

Top again with berry yogurt, toasted walnuts & fresh fruit.
