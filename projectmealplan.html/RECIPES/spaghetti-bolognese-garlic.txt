Spaghetti Bolognese with Garlic, Basil & Oregano

10m
Prep Time

30m
Cook Time

Ingredients 4 Servings
1 medium onion, diced
1 tablespoon Gourmet Garden Garlic, Stir-In Paste
1 lb ground beef, lean
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 can tomatoes, diced
1/4 cup tomato paste
1 tablespoon McCormick Oregano Leaves
1 tablespoon Gourmet Garden Basil
1 package spaghetti
2 ounces Parmesan Cheese

Preparation
Cook onion, Gourmet Garden Garlic Stir-In Paste and gound beef in olive oil.

Add diced tomatoes and tomato paste. Simmer for 30 minutes. Stir in Gourmet Garden Basil Stir-In Paste and oregano leaves.

Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Top with Parmesan Cheese.
