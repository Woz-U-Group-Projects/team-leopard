AVOCADO & PARSLEY SALSA ON FLATBREAD

Description

Insert description

Servings : 6

Ingredients

  • 1/2 small red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 2 tablespoon Gourmet Garden Parsley, Stir-In Paste
  • 1 package flatbread or pitas, cut into triangles and warmed
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Preperation

  1. Mix together the onion, avocado, and Gourmet Garden Parsley Stir-In Paste.
  2. Drizzle with the lime juice and gently stir to combine.
  3. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve the avocado salsa with warmed flatbread cut into triangles.
