Seared Scallops with Garlic and Basil
5m
Prep Time

8m
Cook Time

7

Ingredients 4 Servings
2 to 3 tablespoons olive oil
12 large sea scallops, (about 1 pound)
1 tablespoon Garlic
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon McCormickÂ® Pure Ground Black Pepper
2 tablespoons Basil

Preparation
Brush scallops on both sides with Garlic Paste; sprinkle with salt and pepper. Heat 2 tablespoons of the oil in large skillet on high heat. Place scallops in skillet. Reduce heat to medium. Cook 6 to 8 minutes, until golden brown on both sides, turning only once and adding remaining oil, if needed.

Sprinkle with Basil just before serving. Serve over cooked rice, if desired.
