CREAMY CARIBBEAN COCONUT SMOOTHIE

INGREDIENTS:
– 1 cup vanilla yogurt
– ¼ cup pineapple chunks
– ¼ cup pineapple juice
– 1 tablespoon lime juice
– ¼ teaspoon + 1/8 teaspoon rum extract
– ¼ teaspoon + 1/8 teaspoon coconut extract
– A pinch of allspice
– A sprinkle of lime zest
– A sprinkle of toasted unsweetened coconut
DIRECTIONS:
Blend together 1 cup vanilla yogurt, ¼ cup pineapple chunks, ¼ cup pineapple juice, 1 tablespoon lime juice, ¼ teaspoon + 1/8 teaspoon rum extract, ¼ teaspoon + 1/8 teaspoon coconut extract and a pinch of allspice until smooth. Pour into a glass and sprinkle with lime zest and toasted unsweetened coconut.
