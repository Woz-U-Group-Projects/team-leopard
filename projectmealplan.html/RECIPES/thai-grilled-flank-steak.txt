Thai Grilled Flank Steak
5m
Prep Time

16m
Cook Time

Ingredients 4 Servings
2 tablespoons Thai
2 tablespoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 pound flank steak

Preparation
Mix Thai Seasoning Paste, soy sauce and Worcestershire sauce in a large resealable plastic bag. Add steak; turn to coat. Refrigerate 2 hours or longer for more flavor. Remove steak from marinade. Discard any remaining marinade.

Grill over medium heat 6 to 8 minutes per side or until desired doneness. Let rest 5 minutes. Thinly slice across the grain on a bias to serve.
